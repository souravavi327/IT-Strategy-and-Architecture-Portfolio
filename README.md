# IT Strategy & Enterprise Architecture Portfolio

## Overview
This repository contains a collection of strategic IT business reports and architectural proposals. These projects demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of how to align complex technology deployments with enterprise business objectives, regulatory governance, and financial constraints. 

The portfolio spans three critical domains of modern IT management: **Cloud Infrastructure Migration**, **Cybersecurity Incident Response**, and **Enterprise AI Governance**.

---

## 1. Enterprise Cloud Migration Strategy (Christian Volunteer Services)
**Folder:** `/Cloud-Migration-Strategy`

A £10M strategic proposal for migrating a national organization (260 locations, 4,000 staff) from fragmented legacy on-premise systems to a sustainable cloud infrastructure.
* **Architectural Scope:** Defined the rollout of SaaS (Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365), PaaS (E-Commerce portal), and IaaS (Azure Virtual Machines).
* **Business Process as a Service (BPaaS):** Proposed workflow automations for volunteer onboarding and donor engagement using Power Automate.
* **Information as a Service (INaaS):** Designed a centralized data strategy using Azure Data Lake and Power BI to eliminate data silos.
* **Project Management Focus:** Conducted comprehensive SWOT analysis, budget forecasting (OpEx modeling), and change management strategies to mitigate staff resistance and ensure 99.9% SLA uptime.

## 2. Cybersecurity Incident Response & Zero Trust (loanDepot)
**Folder:** `/Cybersecurity-Incident-Response`

A post-breach recovery strategy and security framework overhaul developed in response to a major financial cyberattack affecting 16.9 million customers.
* **Security Architecture:** Recommended the implementation of a Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA) to prevent lateral movement by threat actors (e.g., Alphv/BlackCat).
* **Process Re-engineering:** Redesigned the Security Incident Response and Data Lifecycle Management processes to ensure rapid containment and compliance.
* **Risk Management:** Established a robust Third-Party Risk Management framework and multi-channel security awareness campaigns to address human-layer vulnerabilities.
* **Executive Communication:** Structured as a briefing for C-level executives (CEO and Senior Management), translating technical cybersecurity threats into actionable business risk mitigation strategies.

## 3. Generative AI Governance & ITIL Integration (APL Apollo Tubes)
**Folder:** `/Enterprise-AI-Governance`

A strategic briefing note on the ethical, legal, and operational deployment of Generative AI within a large-scale manufacturing supply chain.
* **Technology Assessment:** Evaluated GenAI applications for product design, manufacturing process optimization, and supply chain enhancement.
* **Enterprise Governance:** Mapped AI deployment against the ITIL framework, ensuring rigorous service strategy, transition, and continuous improvement protocols.
* **Risk & Compliance:** Addressed critical challenges including data quality, implementation costs, skills gaps, and the social/ethical implications of automated decision-making in an enterprise environment.
